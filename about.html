Being a young mother to Ethan was a struggle, but that has not allowed me to slow down and give up on myself and Ethan. I am glad that we are in a much better position and are both working on our goals. When my youngest son Luke, was diagnosed with congenital heart disease, I was devastated. Yet again, I did not allow that to slow me down and give up on my boys. I work each day to provide my boys the best of me and provide them all I can. My greatest achievement is being a mother to my boys. Whereas I know how difficult life can be, growing up in the foster care system. I was lucky to have been raised by a loving Christian family, and their lifestyle and values I have adopted to how I raise my boys. Now, my goal is to move to a city that will provide my son with the best healthcare.

The Centers for Disease Control & Prevention (CD) website has a lot of information regarding congenital heart disease.
